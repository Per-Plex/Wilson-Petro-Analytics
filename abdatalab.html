About

Wilson Petro-Analytics was established as an independent Canadian consulting firm and has been providing oil and gas reserves
evaluation services to the petroleum industry for the last 16 years. We have consistently produced high quality reserves
evaluations, resource assessments and acquisition advice.

Wilson Petro-Analytics focus is to provide our clients with technical analysis that is reliable and stands the test of time while
providing insights into the assets under evaluation. We work closely with a companies development team and ensure that all the
risks, upsides and technical considerations are taken into account.

We have evaluated oil and gas properties throughout North America and have conducted evaluations in South America, Norway,
UK and Australia. Our evaluations have covered both conventional and unconventional resources.
